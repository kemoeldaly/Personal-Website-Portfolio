Background

I am 29-year-old automotive enthusiast with a new passion for coding. Originally from Suez, Egypt, I was lucky enough to live miles away from the Red Sea beaches until I was 14 years old. After moving and years of adapting, I discovered many interests like cars, motorcycles, and recently, an interest in coding by coincidence through a YouTube video.

I went to college for business but never saw the benefit of finishing it. and I do not regret not finishing my business degree because it would have been a huge waste of money.

I am pursuing my passion by joining Coding Temple to expand my knowledge and skills. Although I studied business in college, I found my true calling in the world of cars and motorcycles, combined with the power of coding, the sky is the limit.

Aside from my technical pursuits, I enjoy taking long walks, spending time with freinds at the beach, working out, playing golf, and exploring everything related to oceans and lakes.
